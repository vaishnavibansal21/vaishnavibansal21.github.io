TAJ MAHAL

Description

A stunning white marble mausoleum in Agra, The Tajmahal was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in Memory of his beloved wife Mumtaj Mahal. It's a Materpiece of Mughal architecture.The Construction work of the Began in 1632.Shah Jahan wanted the world to remember Mumtaj and his love story,that's why he wanted to make some historical heritage in his memory.In which Taj Mahal was built the Construction of the Taj Mahal was completed in about 1643 but still to enhance its beauty and work up to 10 years. The Construction of the Taj Mahal was completed in about 1653 and at that time the cost was approximately 32 million rupees,the Construction of of the Taj Mahal was done by 25,000+ Craftsmen.Ustad Ahmad Lahauri is often regarded as its chief architect.
